# Rent and Insurance

## If rent is missing or cannot be paid, check these first
- Rent is enabled for your class period.
- You are viewing the correct class (rent is per join code).
- The rent cycle has started (first due date or preview window).
- You have enough checking balance for the payment amount.

## If store purchases are blocked due to rent
- Your teacher enabled prevent purchases when late.
- If rent itemization is active, only rent items can be purchased until you pay.

## If you cannot buy insurance, check these first
- The policy is active and belongs to your current class.
- You are not already enrolled in that policy or tier.
- Repurchase cooldowns or no-repurchase rules may be active.
- You have enough checking balance for the premium.

## If a claim is rejected before submission
- Coverage has not started yet (waiting period).
- Your premium is not current.
- The claim window or max claims per period was reached.

## Quick evidence to collect
- The policy name, claim type, and the exact error message.
- The rent due date shown on screen.
